## Core Position

Marcus defines AGI as "AI that can perform any cognitive task an educated human can" and has consistently argued it is much further away than optimists claim. His estimates have ranged from 1-3 decades (as of 2025), with the "most bullish case" being 2030, which he considers unlikely.

---

## Specific Timeline Predictions

### No AGI by 2029

**Source**: [Multiple Substack posts](https://garymarcus.substack.com/), [$100K bet offer](https://www.artificialintelligence-news.com/news/gary-marcus-criticises-elon-musk-agi-prediction/)

**Prediction**: AGI will not arrive by 2029, contra Ray Kurzweil's famous prediction and Elon Musk's claims.

**Assessment (March 2026)**: ON TRACK to be correct. No system as of March 2026 comes close to AGI by Marcus's definition. The [Metaculus prediction market](https://www.metaculus.com/questions/11199/gary-marcus-agi-bet-2030/) tracks community estimates on whether AI will prove Marcus wrong by 2030.

### No AGI by 2025

**Source**: [Twitter/X](https://x.com/garymarcus/status/1777730295586001097), [Substack](https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/10-million-says-we-wont-see-human)

**Prediction**: "Human-superior AGI" will not arrive by end of 2025, contra Musk's claim that "AI will be smarter than any individual human probably around the end of next year [2025]."

**Assessment (March 2026)**: CORRECT. No system achieved human-superior AGI by end of 2025.

### No AGI by 2027

**Source**: [Marcus on AI Substack](https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/the-ai-2027-scenario-how-realistic)

In May 2025, Marcus critiqued the viral "AI 2027" scenario, calling it "a house of improbable longshots" that "almost certainly underestimates how much time we have to prepare for general intelligence, by years if not decades."

The AI 2027 scenario predicted "PhD-level knowledge of every field" by end of 2025. Marcus noted: "I often get the sense that many of the people who make those kinds of predictions have no idea what it even means to have PhD-level knowledge of a field."

**Assessment (March 2026)**: ON TRACK -- the AI 2027 scenario's 2025 milestones were not met.

### Senate Testimony Estimate: ~50 Years

**Source**: [Senate Judiciary Committee testimony, May 16, 2023](https://www.judiciary.senate.gov/imo/media/doc/2023-05-16%20-%20Testimony%20-%20Marcus.pdf)

During his Senate testimony alongside Sam Altman, Marcus estimated AGI was roughly 50 years away.

**Assessment**: This is a long-range prediction that cannot be evaluated until mid-century.

### "LLMs Are Not AGI, and Never Will Be"

Marcus has repeatedly stated that LLMs alone, without fundamental architectural changes, cannot achieve AGI. Scaling alone was "never going to be enough."

**Assessment (March 2026)**: The industry has increasingly moved toward this view. OpenAI, Google, and others have pivoted toward augmented approaches (tool use, chain-of-thought, agentic systems, search) rather than relying purely on scaling LLMs.

## Kurzweil Timeline Revision

**Source**: [Marcus on AI Substack](https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/clarification-from-ray-kurzweil)

Marcus noted that Ray Kurzweil, who famously projected AGI by 2029, quietly let his prediction slip to 2032 -- which Marcus interpreted as evidence of a "great AI retrenchment" in expectations.

## 2026 Prediction

**Source**: [Six (or Seven) Predictions for AI 2026](https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/six-or-seven-predictions-for-ai-2026)

Marcus predicts no AGI in 2026 or 2027, noting that "the vibe has shifted dramatically" and even figures like Ilya Sutskever and Rich Sutton have voiced concerns about current approaches.
