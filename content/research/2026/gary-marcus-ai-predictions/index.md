## Overview

Gary Marcus is a cognitive scientist, NYU Professor Emeritus of Psychology and Neural Science, and one of the most prominent skeptics of current AI approaches. He studied under Steven Pinker, co-founded Geometric Intelligence (acquired by Uber in 2016) and Robust.AI, and authored influential books including *Rebooting AI* (2019) and *Taming Silicon Valley* (2024). He maintains the popular Substack newsletter "Marcus on AI" (474 posts since May 2022) and is a frequent commentator on Twitter/X (@GaryMarcus).

This research compiles his specific, verifiable predictions about AI, organized by category, with assessments of their accuracy as of March 2026.

### Systematic Analysis

A [systematic dataset analysis](https://github.com/davegoldblatt/marcus-claims-dataset) by Dave Goldblatt extracted 2,218 testable claims from all 474 of Marcus's Substack posts (2022-2026). Key findings:

- **Strongest areas**: LLM security vulnerabilities (100% supported), Sora video unreliability (90% supported), agents premature for production (88% supported) -- zero contradictions across these clusters
- **Weakest area**: Market/bubble predictions (27% contradicted in his "GenAI bubble will burst" cluster -- his single worst cluster)
- **Pattern**: His technical predictions are far more accurate than his financial/timing predictions
